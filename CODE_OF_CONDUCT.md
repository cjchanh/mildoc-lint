# Code of Conduct

## Standard

This project is a technical, public-source, local-first linter. Discussion should stay focused on reproducible behavior, source-backed rule boundaries, and synthetic examples.

Participants are expected to:

- use respectful, professional language
- keep reports and examples synthetic
- respect the repository's public/private boundary
- avoid posting sensitive, controlled, customer, or operational material

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- posting CUI, PII, real unit documents, customer data, controlled technical data, operational details, or non-public checklist content
- asking maintainers to make compliance-certification, classification-review, CUI-determination, or tactical-planning claims

## Handling

Maintainers may edit, hide, close, or remove contributions that violate this standard or the repository data boundary. Repeat or serious violations may lead to loss of participation privileges.
